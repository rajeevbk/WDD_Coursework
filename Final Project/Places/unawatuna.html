Unawatuna


This coastal town located in Galle is a famous tourist attraction in Sri lanka. Unawatuna is rich in its biodiversity. The town is filled with entertainment, everywhere you go you will find many ways to enjoy the stay. the place is also famous for its jungle beach, the corals and surfing. If you want to enjoy a day at the beach this would be the best place.
